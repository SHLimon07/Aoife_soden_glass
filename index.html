The optical qualities of glass lend to it a delicacy and a preciousness. Aoife use these qualities in the creation of blown, sculpted and cast glass forms to engage the viewer in emotive responses. The themes running through her artistic work relate to the human condition, memory and nostalgia. These are themes common to every person and for many are highly emotively charged. ​Through manipulation of forms, color and light, in both blown and cast glass pieces Aoife explores these themes in a personal but accessible way. The arrangement of pieces is intended to remain simple, allowing the qualities of the glass to speak. ​In creating her functional glass range Aoife is greatly influenced by Scandinavian design. She uses prefer simple shapes and forms, contrasting bright colours and careful details.
